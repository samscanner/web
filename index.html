RECOGNITION LANGUAGES: English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Swedish,Japanese

Tips to achieve best card recognition:
*Take a picture of a visiting card as clearly as possible. (using anti-shake and placing cards in good lighting condition)
*Take a picture of a visiting card as full as possible. (aligning business card edges to viewfinder edges as closely as possible)
*Be sure to position the mobile on the top of the visiting card at parallel level.
